About Us

Musashi Auto Parts (Musashi) is a global company specializing in the production and supply of high precision parts in the automotive industry. Based in Waterloo, the Technical Centre employs a talented and dynamic team of R&D engineers, CNC technicians, and machine builders who provide unique engineering development activities in design, prototyping and testing and provide support services to our integrated production system in Arthur, Ontario.

Come Join Us Be Unique

Be part of the science in helping us develop smarter internal technologies that will make critical products for the global vehicle market. Come join us and transform your career into something more unique!

At the Technical Centre, were inspired by passionate people who believe in thinking smart and working hard in a harmonious atmosphere to achieve the work they love to fuel the companys global growth. In Musashis Monozukuri culture, we offer an open environment where you can have fun, collaborate and share your unique ideas to make a real business impact on our company and to our customers. We value our people and offer cool perks for working at Musashi including an amazing group benefits plan, a fitness incentive, snow tires incentive, birthday & anniversary gifts, and much more!

About the Job Opportunity - Artificial Intelligence Engineer

In this role, you will be part of a dynamic team who will take on the science of AI/ML and collaborate with various internal teams to help develop smarter internal technologies that will lead to cost reduction and productivity improvements in the Musashi production system. You will be using your knowledge and skills to put the best science into practice and will be constantly learning new technologies to keep up with the market.

Your Responsibilities - What youll be doing:

  • Lead and collaborate with engineering and internal teams to define project scopes, execution plans & schedules and new ideas.
  • Define, develop and test AI/ML models and insights.
  • Conduct applied research to support the development and testing phases of each project.
  • Assess project costs, risks and issues and make recommendations/solutions.
  • Investigate new technologies and make value propositions.
  • Prepare and present written and verbal reports.
  • Travel to other Musashi facilities for projects, meetings, and training.
  • Perform other related duties as required.

About You The Ideal Candidate:

You have what it takes to thrive in a fast-paced automotive company with your passion, ability to think outside of the box, and motivation for success. You have a positive attitude and can adapt to any situation. Youre an effective communicator and collaborate well with others as a team player. You have exceptional organizational and time management skills to effectively execute projects from start to finish. And of course, you are someone who embraces company values and philosophies in all aspect of your work.

Your Qualifications The skills and experience youll bring:

  • Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Math or other related field.
  • 3+ years of demonstrated experience in programming and developing AI/ML implementations.
  • Strong knowledge and implementation ability for computer vision (i.e. CNN) & time series data analysis (i.e. RNN) algorithms.
  • Proven hands-on experience in one or more programming languages an asset - Python, C, C++, Open CV, CUDA C.
  • Strong mathematical and analytical skills.
  • High level of creativity, problem solving and team collaboration ability.
  • Able to travel for business activities.
